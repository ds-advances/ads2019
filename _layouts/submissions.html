To be considered for a short oral presentation or poster please submit an abstract through the meeting's easychair submission page. Abstracts should be prepared on one side of a4 (pdf format, minimum 11pt font) and should describe new or recently published data science research. We will consider submissions which demonstrate advances in data science or AI methodology and/or applications of data science. This year's meeting is themed around four topics: Health & Wellbeing, AI & Robotics, NLP & Text Mining and Security & Privacy.

Talk submission deadline: Friday March 1st
